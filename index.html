About Us

Osom School is an online tuition that will be focussing for children aged 11 to 15 years old to produce digital arts in their free time to be used as a hobby or a career.

Contact Us

Vision

Our goal is to introduce Asian children, especially those in Malaysia, to the world of animation, encouraging their creativity and helping them find joy in creating digital art. We aspire to be the top animation school for young kids in Asia.

Mission

Our goal is to train tutors who excel in communication and working with kids. We prioritize top-notch teaching, strong student connections, and empowering young talents to create their content. Our main focus is teaching animation basics and software skills to students.
